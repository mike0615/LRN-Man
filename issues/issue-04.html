⚙ DMARC / DKIM / SPF — EMAIL AUTHENTICATION
The holy trinity of email authentication. SPF verifies the sending server. DKIM cryptographically signs the message. DMARC tells receiving servers what to do when checks fail: quarantine or reject. Together, they make email domain spoofing impossible — the Duchess's fake CEO emails die before reaching any inbox.

"DMARC reject policy: the phishing campaign ends at the mail server."
⚙ MFA — MULTI-FACTOR AUTHENTICATION
Something you know, something you have, something you are. Even when The Voice tricks a help desk into believing it's the CISO — hardware tokens, authenticator apps, and biometrics can't be reset over the phone. MFA is the wall between a compromised password and a compromised account.

"The password is the username now. MFA is the real lock."
⚙ DEEPFAKE DETECTION AI
Analyzes video and audio streams for synthesis artifacts. Checks lip sync timing (humans within 8ms, fakes 10-15ms off). Scans for missing micro-saccades — the tiny involuntary eye movements genuine humans make. Voice analysis detects AI cloning from pitch variance and breath patterns. Integrated directly into video conferencing platforms.

"A human wouldn't catch it. The AI does."
⚙ OSINT DEFENSE — SOCIAL MEDIA HYGIENE
The Phish King uses open-source intelligence to personalize attacks. Employee social media reveals concerns, schedules, relationships, and vocabulary — all ammunition. OSINT defense: audit what's public, train employees on operational security, and understand that every post is potential spear-phishing material.

"Your public profile is the Phish King's research database."
