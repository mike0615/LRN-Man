# ACT 3: THE NULL

## Scene 1: Planet Maytag -- Homecoming

Planet Maytag's core revealed. Crystalline server towers, rivers of liquid data. At the center, a massive containment sphere where Elder Sudo and Elder Chmod maintain The Null's prison.

> NARRATION: "Planet Maytag. My homeworld. For thirty years, two Elders have held back the fundamental force of digital entropy with nothing but willpower and root access."

**PANEL 2**
LRN Man approaches the containment sphere. Two translucent figures inside -- Elder Sudo and Elder Chmod -- ancient, exhausted, but still fighting.

> ELDER SUDO (weakly): "You came. The signal... reached you."

> THE NULL: "I AM THE SPACE BETWEEN. THE ZERO IN EVERY ONE. THE CORRUPTION IN EVERY SYSTEM. YOU CANNOT DESTROY WHAT IS FUNDAMENTAL."

**PANEL 2**
LRN Man stands against The Null, all 65 tools activated. The five restored Heralds flank him.

> LRN MAN: "You're right. I can't destroy you. Entropy is fundamental. But I can CONTAIN you. Every tool I've ever learned, every battle I've fought -- it was all preparation for this."

**PANEL 3**
The Heralds take their positions -- each one resuming their original Maytag role.

> BREAKOUT: "Container isolation -- restored."
> VENOM REGISTRY: "Image integrity -- verified."
> THE ORCHESTRATOR: "Configuration -- secured."
> CLOUD HYDRA: "Resources -- balanced."
> THE MASQUERADE: "Identities -- authenticated."

## Scene 3: Sealing The Null

**PANEL 1 -- FULL PAGE**
LRN Man channels all 65 tools simultaneously. A massive containment construct forms around The Null.

> LRN MAN: "Sixty-five tools. Six battles. Twenty-three villains faced. Every lesson learned feeds this containment. You don't get to consume anymore."

**PANEL 2**
The Null is sealed. Not destroyed -- contained. A permanent prison of layered security.

> NARRATION: "The Null cannot be destroyed. It is the fundamental entropy of digital systems. But it can be contained -- layer upon layer of security, monitored eternally by the tools and guardians of Maytag."
